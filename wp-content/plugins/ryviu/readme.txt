== Description ==

Ryviu is the best reviews app for the eCommerce and Dropshipping sites. It can help you import all the reviews of products on Aliexpress or Amazon to your store in just a few steps. You can easy to customize reviews with Ryviu and show them in your store. 

Our plugin allows showing reviews from Ryviu to your Woocommerce.

>[Ryviu Docs](http://bit.ly/2woo98O "Ryviu Docs") | [Home Page](https://www.ryviu.com "Ryviu Homepage") | [Contact](https://www.ryviu.com "Contact")

[youtube https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=44jQwV031R8]

### FEATURES:

&#9658; **IMPORT/EXPORT REVIEWS:** You can import reviews from mutil source to Ryviu.

 - Easy import reviews from Aliexpress and Amazon
 - Import reviews from multi-source to Ryviu by CSV file
 - Export reviews from Ryviu to CSV

&#9658; **MANAGER REVIEWS:** You can easily manage reviews with Ryviu Dashboard

 - **Reviews page:** Display, analyze and statistic information of all your reviews
 - **Products page:** Display the list of products on your site, you can add, edit, remove the reviews in here
 - **Question page:** Display and manage all your questions and answers
 - **Media page:** Display and manage all your media file
 - **Settings page:** Settings your account, reviews theme on your store and upgrade your account to premium

&#9658; **DISPLAY REVIEWS TO YOUR SITE:** Customization to match review display with your store theme, from widget colors and have all the functions of app reviews

 - Shortcodes are available for inserting reviews and review form on any page or widget
 - Showing images reviews of customer
 - Allow customer add reviews
 - Reply to reviews to engage with all your customers
 - Shows aggregate reviews microformat
 - Feature reviews page: Show more reviews from multi-product on a page
 - Question and Answer: Allow users to ask questions directly on the product page

== Installation ==

1. You should register an account on [Ryviu](https://www.ryviu.com/pricing "Register Account")
2. Go to [Ryviu settings](https://app.ryviu.io/#/settings "Ryviu settings") page and connect your store with Ryviu
3. Download [Ryviu](https://wordpress.org/plugins/ryviu "Ryviu") plugin from WordPress 
4. Unzip it, upload ryviu folder to the '/wp-content/plugins' directory
